Publish Prototype

You can publish a prototype at any time and share it with others. This creates a snapshot of the prototype design that you can share with others using a shareable link.

Context

From the Prototype page:

Procedure

  1. Select the Publish icon in the toolbar.
  2. Select Publish. A URL to your prototype displays. Anyone with this link can access your prototype.

    Copy the link and share with others to get feedback on your prototype.
