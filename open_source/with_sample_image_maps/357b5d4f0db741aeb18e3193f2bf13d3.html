Design Learning Center

Use the Learning Center to see practical design methods, examples, and templates from design experts.

Select Learning from the header to view the Learning Center on the SPLASH home page or from BUILD. The Learning Center includes these useful resources for anyone creating applications:

  • Design Method Cards: Need information on Design Thinking and the design process? Method cards have information on the design process and best practices. They include examples, templates, and steps to follow during different phases of your product cycle. These phases include Interviewing Users, Creating Personas, Storyboarding and more.

  • Resources: Want to learn more? Resources, such as SAP Fiori Design Guidelines and courses in Developing Software using Design Thinking and Design for Non-Designers.
  • Design Services: Need help? Get information on SAP User Experience Design Services.
