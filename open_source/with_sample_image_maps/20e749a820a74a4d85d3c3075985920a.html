Report Issues to Support

You can report issues with Splash and BUILD to the teams at contact.build@sap.com. S-Users can create a ticket in the BCP Customer Support system.

The Splash and BUILD teams make a big effort to ensure your experience with Splash and BUILD is seamless and error free. However, in the event you need support, you can contact the teams directly on contact.build@sap.com.

If your organization has an SAP Business Continuity Plan, or BCP, your S-User can create an support ticket in the BCP Customer Support system from https://support.sap.com/home.html.
